The negativity bias implies that negative thoughts have a bigger impact on shaping behavior. The brain pays more attention to negative memories partly because they are more complex. From an evolutionary standpoint, this seems to be a survival mechanism. The brain takes more time to assess negativity in order to ensure that it knows how to handle the problem in the future. Thus, we are all doomed to have more negative than positive thoughts. That's why we created Black Hole. Black Hole allows you to engage negative thoughts by writing them down and then releasing those thoughts into the Universe.
